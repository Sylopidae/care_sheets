Species: Chromatopelma cyanopubescens

• Humidity: Ambient (~50%)
• Temperature: ~72°F
• Habit: Semi-arboreal
• Feeding schedule: Twice Weekly
• Growth rate: Medium
• Temperament: Docile

This species is a heavy webber, and will construct an above-ground 'burrow' of sorts with webbing. A busy tank with plants and hardscape is appreciated by the subadults and adults of this species.
